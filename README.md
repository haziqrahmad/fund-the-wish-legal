# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 10 June 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Fund the Wish** mobile application ("the App", "we", "our") handles your information. By using the App, you agree to the practices described below.

## Summary

Fund the Wish is a **local-first** personal finance app. All your financial data — entries, wishes, balances, scenarios — is stored **only on your device**. We do not run a server. We do not have your data. We never see it.

## Data we collect

**We do not collect any personal data.** The App does not have user accounts, does not require sign-in, and does not transmit your financial information off your device.

## Data stored on your device

The following data is stored locally on your device, in an SQLite database and the App's private file storage:

- Financial entries (income, expenses, debts, investments)
- Wishes, milestones, and what-if scenarios
- Categories, currency preference, and other settings
- Optional wish hero images you pick from your photo library

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

## Device permissions

The App may request the following permissions:

- **Photo library access** — only when you choose to attach an image to a wish. The image is copied to the App's private storage and is never transmitted off your device.

## Network requests

The App makes outbound network requests in one case:

- When you paste a URL into a wish, the App fetches that page's public metadata (Open Graph image and title) to display a preview. The request goes from your device directly to the URL you provided. We do not log, store, or proxy these requests.

## Third-party services

The App does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, crash reporting services (as of this version), or any other third-party data collection tools.

## Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone.

## Data deletion

Because all your data is stored on your device, you can delete it at any time by:
1. Returning to the App's starting screen (via Settings → **Back to starting screen**) and tapping the **trash icon** next to "Continue with…" to clear your planner data, or
2. Deleting the App from your device.

There is nothing for us to delete on our end, because we never had it.

## Changes to this policy

If we materially change this Privacy Policy in a future update, we will update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after a change constitutes acceptance.
